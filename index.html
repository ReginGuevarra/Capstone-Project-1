Assist, originally known by various names such as "Sell Sneakers Here," "Kickfeed," and "Discounted Kicks" on Instagram, has undergone significant changes over time. Since 2018, the brand has transformed into a physical store located in Hayward, California, where it has developed strong relationships with both clients and business partners.

Thanks to its customer-focused approach, Assist has earned a reputation for being flexible and accommodating. The store's clients appreciate the convenience of being able to purchase products either online or in-person, which has contributed to high levels of customer satisfaction. Overall, Assist's evolution from a social media page to a physical store has allowed it to build a loyal following of customers who appreciate the brand's dedication to providing excellent service and quality products.
